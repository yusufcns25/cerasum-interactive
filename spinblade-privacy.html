SpinBlade Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

SpinBlade does not collect personally identifiable information from users.

Data Collection

The app may collect anonymous usage data through third-party services such as Google Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob. This data may include device information, app performance data, and general usage statistics.

Advertising

SpinBlade uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use data according to its own privacy policies.

Data Security

All collected data is transmitted securely and is not sold to third parties.

Children’s Privacy

SpinBlade is not specifically directed at children under 13 years of age.
